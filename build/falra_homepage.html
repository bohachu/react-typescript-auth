About Falra Distributed Framework

Falra Distributed Framework(FDA) is a tool that enables software developers to easily utilize resources from multiple hosts and quickly process massive amounts of data.

  • Faster

    A dataset of 10 TB that originally required 100 days of computation can now be processed in just one day, resulting in a 700-fold acceleration.

  • Automatically Distributed Computing

    Previously, it took 120 hours to process tasks on one machine. However, after automatically distributing the tasks to 700 machines, the time required was reduced to just one hour.

  • Vast Storage

    For only $19.9, you can have 1 TB of storage space, and there are no additional charges for uploading or downloading.
