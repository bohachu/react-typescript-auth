Falra Distributed Framework (FDF)

Hello! We are the CAMEO team, and through this questionnaire, we would like to understand users' feedback on our new product, Falra Distributed Framework (FDF).

In the past, Taiwan Environmental Protection Administration IoT Data Center used a combination of databases such as Hbase, Redis, and Elasticsearch to provide third-party platforms with massive IoT data for integration and application. However, none of them were as fast and stable as the massive data service (Falra Distributed Framework) introduced by Cameo.

Over the past six years, Cameo has accumulated over 10TB of IoT historical CSV data from the Taiwan Environmental Protection Administration. Using the common Python method for slicing data, it would take 100 days to complete. However, with the Falra Distributed Framework technology, Cameo is now able to finish the data processing in just one day. By utilizing this technology, Cameo can save time and help customers focus on analyzing the most important data information, creating value, rather than just processing the data itself.

The product advantages of FDF include:

  • Price capping: Effectively controls the cost of developer environment
  • Automatic distributed computation: IoT data volume can be computed in just 10 minutes instead of 4 days, accelerating 700 times
  • Large storage space: Compared to BigQuery, storage, uploading, and downloading all require payment, but FDF allows for storage of up to 1TB within the service.

We hope to collect your user experience, feedback, and suggestions on these product advantages through this questionnaire, so that the team can understand how to improve the product in the future.
